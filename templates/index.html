SYSTEM MANUAL & USAGE GUIDE

What is NewsWeave?

NewsWeave is an autonomous Forensic Intelligence Agent. Unlike standard search engines that provide links, NewsWeave reads, verifies, and synthesizes data into a comprehensive dossier. It uses a multi-vector search engine to cross-reference facts, debunk rumors, and gather real-time market data.

How to Operate

  1. Select Region (): Click the Globe icon. Choose your target country (e.g., 'India' for INR currency & local laws, 'USA' for federal data).
  2. Select Intelligence Mode (): Click the Brain icon. Choose how the AI should think (see modes below).
  3. Input Parameter: Type your query in the Superbar. Be specific for best results.
  4. Execute: Click the Blue Arrow to launch the investigation.

Intelligence Modes

  • Auto: The AI analyzes your prompt and picks the best strategy automatically.
  • Catalog / List: Forces the AI to generate exhaustive lists (e.g., "List all new cyber crimes").
  • Fact Check: rigorous verification protocol. Perfect for rumors, hoaxes, or "too good to be true" news.
  • Market Analysis: Hunts for financial tickers, revenue reports, and economic growth stats.
  • Deep Research: The standard "All-Rounder" mode for deep context and history.
