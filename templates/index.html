What is NewsWeave?

NewsWeave is an autonomous Forensic Intelligence Agent. Unlike standard search engines that provide links, NewsWeave reads, verifies, and synthesizes data into a comprehensive dossier.

Intelligence Modes

  • Auto: The AI analyzes your prompt and picks the best strategy automatically.
  • Catalog / List: Forces the AI to generate exhaustive lists (e.g., "List all new cyber crimes").
  • Fact Check: rigorous verification protocol. Perfect for rumors, hoaxes, or "too good to be true" news.
  • Market Analysis: Hunts for financial tickers, revenue reports, and economic growth stats.
  • Deep Research: The standard "All-Rounder" mode for deep context and history.
